ELI

EspritLifeInsurrence

ELI is a digital application that simplifies the management of your insurance remotely. It allows you to subscribe to several products (auto, home, travel, life, etc.), pay your premiums, track your claims, and instantly generate online quotes. Its intuitive interface ensures fast and secure access to all your services. All documents (contracts, certificates) are instantly available from your smartphone. My STAR fully centralizes and digitizes your insurance experience.
